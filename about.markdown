This site is a collection of technical notes, expository writing, and documentation of projects from my studies and personal work. It serves as a place where I write about what I build and what I learn in the process.

I hold a dual Master’s degree in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science from Eindhoven University of Technology. During my thesis project, I realized I had little experience with technical writing beyond constructing relatively small mathematical models and presenting formulae. This gave me a renewed appreciation for the quality of scientific and technical literature that I had previously only been consuming.

The main purpose of this site is to practice technical writing and to organize my thoughts. Of course---since I publish it online---I also hope that some of the material is useful or interesting to others.
